CMS Issues Moratorium on New DME Supplier Medicare Enrollment

The Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services has enacted a sweeping moratorium halting all new durable medical equipment supplier enrollments in Medicare programs. The freeze directly impacts the competitive landscape for DME providers of record — preventing new entrants while existing suppliers retain their licensing status. No timeline for lifting the moratorium has been announced.
